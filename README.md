# SQL Business Intelligence Case Study

## Objective
Analyze retail business performance using SQL queries 
to answer real business questions and drive decisions.

## Tools Used
Python · SQLite · Pandas · Matplotlib · Jupyter Notebook

## Database Structure
- Customers Table — 500 customers across 4 regions
- Products Table — 15 products across 3 categories
- Orders Table — 2,000 orders over 2 years

## Business Questions Answered
1. What is the overall revenue and profit performance?
2. Which product category generates the most revenue?
3. What are the top 5 best selling products?
4. Which region performs best in sales?
5. What is the monthly revenue trend?
6. Which customer segment is most valuable?
7. How do discounts impact profit margins?

## Key Findings
- Technology is the top revenue category
- Higher discounts heavily reduce profit margins
- Corporate segment has highest revenue per customer
- All regions show consistent performance
